Mount Abu the mountain forms a rocky plateau 22 km long by 9 km wide. The highest peak on the mountain is Guru Shikhar at 1,722 m (5,650 ft) above sea level. It is referred to as 'an oasis in the desert' as its heights are home to rivers, lakes, waterfalls and evergreen forests.
It is also home to numerous Hindu and Jain temples. The nearest train station is Abu Road railway station 28 km away.
